## How to Use This Journal

Add entries when you observe:

| Tag | When to Use |
|-----|-------------|
| `[MANUAL]` | Did something by hand that could be scripted |
| `[REPETITIVE]` | Did something that's common across projects |
| `[FRICTION]` | Hit a snag, needed workaround, unexpected complexity |
| `[INSIGHT]` | Learned something worth remembering |
| `[DECISION]` | Made a choice between alternatives |

### Entry Format

```markdown
### YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM - [TAG] Short title
**Context:** What were you trying to do?
**Action:** What did you actually do?
**Time spent:** Estimate
**Automation opportunity:** None | Low | Medium | High
**Notes:** Any additional context
```
